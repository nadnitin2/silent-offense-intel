1. The Architectural Edge of IPSet vs. Standard Rules

By switching to IPSet, rule lookup complexity drops from sequential iteration $O(n)$ down to an optimized constant-time $O(1)$ constraint. This prevents CPU exhaustion attacks under heavy DDoS vector injection, maintaining firewall integrity natively.
